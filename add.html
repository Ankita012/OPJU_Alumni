Alumni meet

The Alumni meet is a time for homecoming to our alma mater, to connect and renew bonds of old friendships. It is also an opportunity to participate in the timeless Indian tradition of Gurudakshina, which goes back to the age of the sages.

We all have gained tremendously from the quality of education OPJU imparted, and a large part of our success can be traced back to the opportunities our OPJU education created for us. Further, all that was done at ridiculously subsidized rates.

We request you to think in terms of giving back one day of income for each year since we graduated. Or, roughly a month of your current gross income.How much you give is important, but what is even more important is that each and every one of us contributes.
